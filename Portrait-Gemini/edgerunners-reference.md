**Vibe:** Teal, emerald green — Freelance mercenaries living on the razor's edge between a corpo bullet and a gutter grave. No flag, no loyalty — just skill, chrome, and whoever's paying the most eddies this week. They jack in, gear up, and disappear into Night City's neon labyrinth. Teal light reflecting off wet chrome, the hum of a cyberdeck booting up in a rain-soaked alley, the click of a magazine sliding home. Every job might be the last one.
